Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, established in 1997, is a renowned institution affiliated with Anna University. It offers a wide range of engineering programs and is accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade. The college is known for its strong academic curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a dedicated faculty. REC has a robust placement record, with strong industry connections that provide students with excellent opportunities for internships and jobs. The campus also fosters a vibrant student life with various extracurricular activities and clubs.
